% ============ SLIDE 2: WHAT ============
\begin{frame}{What We Are Proposing}
  \framelogo
  \small
  A self-contained device that photographs a plant, identifies its species, assesses its health,
  and fuses that with live soil sensors to give a \textbf{daily care recommendation} --- all
  running on the Arduino UNO Q, no cloud.

  \vspace{4pt}
  \textbf{\color{ucsdblue}Where the AI/ML runs (on the AUQ):}
  \begin{itemize}
    \item Two TFLite CNNs execute \textbf{on the UNO Q Ubuntu MPU}: \emph{species ID} and
          \emph{health classification} (healthy / stressed / diseased).
    \item A fusion + rule layer combines model output with soil moisture \& pH against
          per-species ideal ranges to generate the recommendation --- also on-device.
  \end{itemize}

  \vspace{2pt}
  \begin{columns}[T]
    \begin{column}{0.5\textwidth}
      \textbf{\color{ucsdblue}Must haves}
      \begin{itemize}\small
        \item On-device species classification
        \item On-device health classification
        \item Live soil moisture + pH read on STM32
        \item Fused daily care recommendation
      \end{itemize}
    \end{column}
    \begin{column}{0.5\textwidth}
      \textbf{\color{ucsdblue}Nice to haves}
      \begin{itemize}\small
        \item Fine-tuning on our own plant photos
        \item Trend tracking / history dashboard
        \item Live display output (MIPI-DSI)
        \item Small on-device LLM for phrasing advice
      \end{itemize}
    \end{column}
  \end{columns}
\end{frame}
